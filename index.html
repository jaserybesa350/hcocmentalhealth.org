Empowering Therapists Through Education & Self-Care

HCOC MENTAL HEALTH CORPORATION is a 501(c)(3) charitable non-profit dedicated to providing high-quality educational resources, continuing education, and self-care support for mental health therapists across New York and beyond.

Therapist Resources & Continuing Education

Our Core Offerings for Mental Health Practitioners

We provide tailored educational resources and support to help therapists advance their practice, maintain professional development, and prioritize their own mental health and self-care:

  • Continuing Education: Accessible, relevant CE courses aligned with mental health industry standards and best practices.
  • Self-Care Support: Curated resources, workshops, and tools to help therapists build sustainable self-care routines.
  • Educational Materials: Comprehensive guides, webinars, and resources for evidence-based therapeutic practice.
  • Community Connection: Opportunities to connect with fellow therapists for peer support and knowledge sharing.

About HCOC Mental Health Corporation

Our Mission & Charitable Purpose

Founded in 2025, HCOC MENTAL HEALTH CORPORATION is a New York-based 501(c)(3) charitable non-profit organized under the New York Not-for-Profit Corporation Law. Our core mission is to provide high-quality educational resources, continuing education opportunities, and self-care support to mental health therapists—with no focus on providing licensed professional services.

We are committed to advancing the practice of mental health care by equipping therapists with the tools, knowledge, and support they need to deliver exceptional care to their clients, while also prioritizing the therapists' own mental health and professional well-being.

All our efforts are exclusively charitable and aligned with Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code, with no part of our net earnings inuring to the benefit of private individuals.
